One of Chocolatey's greatest strengths is the Chocolatey Community Repository; a package repository with over 10,000 pre-made packages that do everything from install software to configure machines.

Chocolatey for Business gives its users the ability to leverage those packages in their own internal repositories without needing to reach out over the internet via Package Internalizer, ensuring their usability even in air-gapped environments.
Combined with an automation platform, Package Internalizer can take the packages you want, internalize them, and push them to your internal repository, then update those packages on a schedule of your choosing.

To setup and configure an automation platform, please select a tile to get started:
